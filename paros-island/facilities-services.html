Facilities & Services

In- room Amenities

Albatross guest rooms are designed to feel as good as they look. Each of our hotel rooms comes with such amenities as:

  • A/C
  • Mini Bar
  • Satellite TV
  • Direct-dial phone
  • 2-channel Music
  • Private bathroom with shower
  • Hair-Dryer
  • Private Balcony or Terrace with Sea/ Pool, Garden or Mountain View

Facilities & Services

Albatross hotel provides a wide range of facilities and services that will delightfully surprise you and offer you a happy, relaxing, worry-free holiday.

  • Sweet water swimming pool
  • Umbrellas /sun beds / towels for the pool free of charge
  • Pool/ snack bar
  • Lounge area equipped with a satellite TV, large DVD and Book library and table games
  • Children’s playground
  • Extensive gardens
  • Parking area
  • Safety deposit boxes free of charge
  • Fax / Mail service
  • Free Wi-Fi Internet Access around the swimming pool area
  • Car / Motorbike / Bicycle rental
  • Daily cruises to Delos-Mykonos, Santorini, and Small Cyclades
  • Activities planning: trekking, mountain biking, horse riding, wind/ kite surf, scuba diving, snorkeling, sailing, sea kayaking etc
  • Special Events Hosting: wedding receptions, birthday parties etc
  • Massage therapies upon request
  • Doctor on call
  • Babysitter upon request
  • Pet friendly accommodation
