Location

Albatross hotel is situated on the south-eastern part of Paros Island in Logaras village and only 200 meters away from the sandy, awarded with a Blue Flag Logaras beach.

Logaras offers amenities such as mini markets, grocery store, internet café, tavernas, cafes and bars that greet you with a unique atmosphere of hospitality.

Nearby Punda beach –awarded with a Blue flag as well- combines water sports, dance music and entertainment in an idyllic situation.

Fifteen minutes walk along the coastline brings you to the peaceful fishing village of Piso Livadi and its pretty harbour around which the local life centers.

Within a walking distance from Logaras there is one of the most beautiful villages in Paros, Marpissa whose architecture and lay out are of particular interest.
