Logaras Beach

Logaras village and the awarded with a Blue Flag Logaras Beach.
Looking around the beautiful Cycladic island of Paros, one can be forgiven for thinking there is nothing beyond the popular, busy resorts of Parikia and Naoussa. Venture however further south to the tiny picturesque village of Logaras with its idyllic sandy beach, shaded by tamarisk trees and discover a paradise in miniature.
Within a walking distance from Marpissa, Piso Livadi and Pounda Beach , Logaras will charm you with its relaxing and friendly atmosphere as well as its “flavours”. On Logaras beach you can enjoy fresh seafood together with local mezedes and wine at cozy tavernas and bars with great views of Naxos and Iraklia islands.
